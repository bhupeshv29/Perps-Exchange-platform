//notes
Three prices you'll usually see
Last Price
    The price of the most recent trade.
    Used for charting and order execution.
Mark Price
    Fair-value price used for PnL and liquidation.
    Usually smoother than the last price.
Index Price
    A weighted average of spot prices from multiple exchanges.
    One of the inputs used to calculate the mark price.
Quick rule
        Orders execute at the market/last price.
        Liquidation happens based on the mark price.
        Unrealized PnL is usually calculated using the mark price.
